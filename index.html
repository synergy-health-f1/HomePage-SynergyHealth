About Us

Synergy Health Digital exists to revolutionize the digitalization of health records in Africa. Our goal is to provide the enabling digital platform that will massively improve the overall productivity and effectiveness of the healthcare system while empowering patients to have easy access to their health records, and interact with healthcare providers anytime and anywhere through electronic devices.

Our software system is designed with reliability, flexibility, scalability and agility in mind. Our software system which chronicles the entire electronic health journey of patients is used in government hospitals, academic medical centers, private clinics and multi-specialty groups. We demonstrate our patient-centric healthcare system by making our software available in the homes and pockets of our patients.

The focus of our team of experienced and certified project management experts, seasoned software developers and business analysts is to work together to deliver innovative business value to our healthcare providers and patients.
